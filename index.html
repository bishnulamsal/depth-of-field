Photographer's Field Guide
Rule

Sunny 16

Sunny day: ƒ/16, shutter = 1/ISO. ISO 200 → 1/200 s.

Stability

1/Focal Length

Handheld minimum shutter = 1÷focal length. 85mm → 1/85s min.

Golden Hour

Magic Light

Shoot within 1 hr of sunrise/sunset. Warm directional light.

Portrait

Aperture Sweet Spot

ƒ/1.4–2 for bokeh. ƒ/2.8–4 keeps both eyes sharp.

Landscape

Hyperfocal

Focus at hyperfocal: from half that to ∞ stays sharp.

Motion

Freeze vs Blur

Freeze: 1/500–1/2000s. Creative blur: 1/4–4s on tripod.

Noise

ISO Sweet Spots

100–400: clean. 800–3200: good FF. 6400+: accept grain.

Composition

Rule of Thirds

Place subject at grid intersections. Horizon on top/bottom third.

White Balance

Colour Temp

Shade 7500K · Overcast 6500K · Day 5500K · Tungsten 3200K

Street

Zone Focus

Pre-focus 3m, ƒ/8, hip-level. Zone 2–6m sharp — no AF lag.

Macro

Close-Up DoF

At 1:1 magnification DoF under 1mm at ƒ/2.8. Stack to be safe.

Storage

Two Card Rule

Split across two cards — a failed 256GB loses more than 64GB.
